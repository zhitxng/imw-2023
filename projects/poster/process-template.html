P2 Process

Ideation

Wildfires immediately came to mind when I read the details of this talk. Given its current relevance in the evolution of the climate crisis, I thought it would be fitting to base the design of the site around this topic. Additionally, wood has been an extremely important material when it comes to Architecture, Landscape, and Design.

I was especially inspired by this image because it has a shockingly beautiful and graphic quality to it, despite being devastating. The colour palette for this project was inspired by these muted tones.

As for typography, I knew I wanted to use a wood type to pay homage to the theme. Champion Gothic Heavyweight was used as it’s a‘cleaned-up version’ of the original Gothic type cut by Edwin Allen. Paired with Adobe Caslon, it’s meant to showcase the delicacy of nature hidden amongst the ruggedness.

Execution

The layout was inspired by a news headline in addition to the idea of layers (layers of earth, layers of wood, layers of pollution etc.) Thus, all the elements sort of stack up on top of each other.

The animation overlay was inspired by ash flowing in the wind, I used an animation template off codepen(unfortunately lost the link). The blockiness of the title becomes a great background to bring out these white particles.
